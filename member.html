Research Summary

I am interested in the circuitry that biological systems use to integrate information from physical cues into a gene expression program appropriate to their circumstance. I am currently using ribosome profiling, developed in Jonathan Weissman's laboratory at UCSF, to determine how genome-wide translation adapts to temperature shifts in E. coli.
